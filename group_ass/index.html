Convict Record


Convict register (chronological) 1824 to 1839


These indexes were compiled from the Chronological register of convicts (Crown prisoners) at Moreton Bay penal settlement as kept by the Commandant's Office, Moreton Bay, from 1824 to 1839. The register is divided into three main sections: a (Part 1) Chronological Register of Convicts by ship's arrival date, with an alphabetical index by convict name in the front of the register. (Part 2) List of Prisoners in the Ordinary Service of the Crown at Moreton Bay October 21st, 1839. (Part 3) an Alphabetical listing by surname of the prisoners' personal details including height, complexion, eye and hair colour, age, religion and native place.

Data Visualization Project


Project Conviction Record provides three different types of the data visualizations. One of them is the map visualization of England, which gives that each suburb of England had how many convicts there are. Second one is the data summary of conviction data set.There are several diagrams and discription of Conviction data set to find the features. Last one is Shipping map, which able to track the route from England to Australia of ships in response to each suburb of England. They help you to find features of conviction record clearly.
